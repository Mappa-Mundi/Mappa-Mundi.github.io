Our Story

Mappa Mundi was born from a simple yet powerful idea: what if we could make history accessible, engaging, and personal?

The name "Mappa Mundi" comes from the Latin phrase meaning "map of the world," reflecting our mission to map and preserve the world's historical heritage for future generations.

Founded by Ian Haynes, a passionate history enthusiast and software engineer, Mappa Mundi combines cutting-edge technology with a deep appreciation for historical preservation. Ian's dual passions for historical exploration and technological innovation drove him to create a platform that effectively intertwines the threads of time and space.

What started as a personal project has grown into a comprehensive platform that helps thousands of users discover the rich historical tapestry that surrounds them every day.

Our Mission

At Mappa Mundi, we believe that history is not just found in textbooks, but all around us.

Our mission is to make historical discovery accessible, engaging, and personal. We aim to transform the way people perceive and interact with their surroundings by connecting them with the historical sites in their vicinity.

We strive to:

  • Preserve History: Document and preserve historical locations for future generations
  • Educate: Make historical learning engaging and interactive
  • Connect: Help people develop a deeper connection with their local and global heritage
  • Inspire: Encourage exploration and curiosity about the past

By placing historical events geographically and offering an immersive narrative experience, we hope to foster a deeper understanding and appreciation for the rich tapestry of human history.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Accuracy

We are committed to providing accurate, well-researched historical information. Every location in our database is carefully verified and sourced from reliable historical records.

Accessibility

We believe that historical knowledge should be accessible to everyone. Our app is designed to be user-friendly and available to people of all ages and backgrounds.

Preservation

We are dedicated to preserving historical knowledge and locations. By documenting these sites, we help ensure that important historical information is not lost to time.

Our Technology

Mappa Mundi leverages cutting-edge technology to provide users with an exceptional historical discovery experience.

  • Advanced Mapping: High-quality maps with precise location data
  • Comprehensive Database: Extensive collection of historical sites and information
  • Mobile Optimization: Designed specifically for mobile devices and on-the-go exploration
  • Offline Capabilities: Access historical information even without internet connectivity
  • Regular Updates: Continuous improvements and new location additions

Our technology stack is designed to be reliable, fast, and user-friendly, ensuring that users can focus on discovering history rather than dealing with technical issues.

Our Vision for the Future

As we look to the future, Mappa Mundi aims to become the world's most comprehensive platform for historical discovery.

Our vision includes:

  • Global Expansion: Expanding our database to include historical sites from around the world
  • Enhanced Features: Adding virtual reality experiences, augmented reality overlays, and interactive historical reenactments
  • Community Features: Allowing users to contribute their own historical discoveries and stories
  • Educational Partnerships: Collaborating with schools, museums, and historical societies
  • Preservation Initiatives: Supporting efforts to preserve and protect historical sites

We believe that by making history more accessible and engaging, we can inspire a new generation of history enthusiasts and help preserve our shared heritage for future generations.
