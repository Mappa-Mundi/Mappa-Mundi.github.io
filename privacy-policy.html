Mappa Mundi - Privacy Policy

At Mappa Mundi, we respect your privacy and commit to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Company Name ("we," "us," "our") handles your Personal Information, including your location data. Please read this policy carefully to understand our data practices. Your use of our services constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

To improve the user experience and enable certain features, we collect location data when you use our services. The location data collected is ephemeral on our servers, meaning it's only temporarily stored.

3. How We Collect Information

Our services may use technologies like GPS, Wi-Fi, or IP addresses to determine your device's approximate location. The collection of location data happens only with your explicit consent.

4. Use of Information

While the location data we collect is ephemeral on our servers, it is used to enhance your user experience and may be used for advertising purposes. We utilize Google AdMob for our advertising services.

5. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display ads in our services. Google AdMob may use your location data to show you personalized ads. Their use of information is guided by Google's Privacy Policy, which we recommend you review.

6. Retention of Information

Given the ephemeral nature of the location data we collect, we do not retain this information on our servers beyond the necessary period required for its collection. Once the data collection process is completed, the data is automatically deleted from our servers.

7. Security of Information

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices, along with security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

8. Consent and Control

We only collect location data with your explicit consent. You can revoke this consent at any time through your device or application settings, but please note that some features of our services might not function properly without this data.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
