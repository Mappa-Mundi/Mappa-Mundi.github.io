Mappa Mundi

Mapping the past, into the present

Unlock the splendid mosaic of the past around you with Mappa Mundi, your definitive companion for historical exploration. Plunge into the compelling tales of bygone eras, monumental landmarks, influential personalities, and dramatic conflicts that have indelibly carved the contours of our present world. With Mappa Mundi, history doesn't just echo - it resonates, right in the palm of your hand.

Our Mission

Our mission at Mappa Mundi is to illuminate the rich, layered fabric of history that lies beneath our everyday landscapes. We aim to make learning about the past an engaging, interactive journey rather than a mere recitation of facts. By placing historical events geographically and offering an immersive narrative experience, we hope to foster a deeper connection between individuals and their surroundings.

Our goal is to inspire curiosity, broaden understanding, and encourage exploration, transforming the way our users perceive and interact with the world. Mappa Mundi is not just about discovering history - it's about living it.

Our Story

Mappa Mundi is the brainchild of Ian Haynes, a fervent history aficionado and software engineer. His dual passions for historical exploration and technological innovation spurred him on a mission to construct a distinctive platform, effectively intertwining the threads of time and space. Driven by the timeless Latin axiom that signifies 'a map of the world', Mappa Mundi was meticulously crafted and ultimately actualized, embodying a convergence of the past and the present in a truly unique manner.
